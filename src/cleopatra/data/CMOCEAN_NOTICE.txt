Attribution for cmocean_presets.json
=====================================

The colour ramps in `cmocean_presets.json` are derived from the cmocean
oceanography colormap collection:

    https://github.com/matplotlib/cmocean
    Copyright (c) 2015 Kristen M. Thyng

Only cmocean's colour *data* is used: a curated subset of its colormaps was
sampled to hex control points (see `_meta.n_points` in the JSON) and paired
with ocean/hydrology/DEM variable labels to form cleopatra DATA_STYLES presets.
No cmocean code is included or used at runtime.
