    VectorGlyph¶

    VectorGlyph renders a 2-D (u, v) field over (x, y) positions as arrows (quiver), wind barbs (barbs), or streamlines (streamplot), coloured by the per-vector magnitude hypot(u, v).

u, v = np.cos(x * np.pi), np.sin(y * np.pi)
